How to Use

Purpose

The purpose of this game is to encourage users to adapt a more sustainable lifestyle. The community built in this game is reflective of what reality could be when people make more sustainable choices.

Points

To earn points, users must upload a photo of sustainable actions. To do this, click on upload and tap on the drop down menu for whichever category your sustaiable action falls under. Select the action and attach your image. Click submit. You will earn points for every photo uploaded!
